I was in the Drama club at Whitefish High School. I joined the drama club in my junior year, with the winter production of It's a Wonderful Life, where I played Violet. Our production of It's a Wonderful Life was a radio play based on the movie of the same name. I then worked as the stage manager for their state showcase production of Everything I Need To Know, I Learned In Kindergarten, which we performed in early 2019. This show was based on a book of the same name. In my senior year, I was one of the main characters in the showcase production of The Wolves. After the performance of The Wolves in early 2020, I participated in the spring production of M*A*S*H, where I played multiple smaller parts (due to the overlap between the preparation to perform The Wolves and auditions for M*A*S*H). In our production, I played a few background characters, as well as both Congresswoman Goldfarb and Mitzi (one of the Bonwit sisters). Though I truly enjoyed performing, my favorite part of the drama club was being able to work with all my friends to create an excellent show that we could be proud of.
