I graduated from high school in 2020 and am continuing my education at FVCC by studying Web Technology. I took classes in both Java and Python during High School through FVCC's Running Start program. While in school, I participated in the drama club. I worked both backstage and as an actor. Our drama club participated in the state showcase each year, and I went in both 2019 and 2020. I spent time each week volunteering at Stumptown Art Studio throughout my high school career. I would help by organizing new inventory, helping set up for parties, and generally act as an extra set of hands. I've also taken a variety of web-focused courses through my high school, including introduction courses to Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe Animate, and Adobe Premiere. Though I've worked in both Java and Python, this is my first experience with HTML. I've already learned a lot throughout this course, and I'm looking forward to learning more in Interactive Web 2.
