Listening
Home Education Drama Coding

I've always loved all kinds of music, though I tend to prefer songs that tell stories. Some of my favorite artists that do this are Alec Benjamin, Cavetown, AJR, and Hozier. I also really love an artist that plays with words; a great example of this would be Cosmo Sheldrake. Several of his songs incorporate phrases from children's rhymes in new ways. I've always believed that a great way to get to know someone is to get to know their music taste. What someone values in music helps to highlight what they value in life. As a way to include this belief in my website, I've provided a list of artists and songs that I enjoy. This list doesn't include everything, but it does include several songs and artists that I've listened to recently and that I enjoy. Keep in mind that while music is a great way to get to know someone, different people interpret music and lyrics differently.
